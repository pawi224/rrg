The Red River Gorge is a canyon system in the Daniel Boone National Forest. It is famous for its abundunce of natural arches, cliffs, and waterfalls. Its most famous arch, Natural Bridge, has a state resort park dedicated to it.


The Red River Gorge is located in the eastern part of Kentucky.
